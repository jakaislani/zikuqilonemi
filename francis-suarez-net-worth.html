Francis Suarez Net Worth: How Much is Francis Suarez Worth? – Francis Xavier Suarez is an accomplished American lawyer and politician who currently serves as the 43rd mayor of Miami, Florida.

Born on October 6, 1977, Suarez has made significant contributions to public service and has established himself as a prominent figure in Miami’s political landscape.

Suarez’s political journey began when he was elected as the City of Miami Commissioner for District 4 in a runoff election on November 17, 2009. He successfully held this position until his election as the mayor of Miami in 2017, securing a second term in 2021. It is worth noting that although Suarez is a registered Republican, the position of Miami mayor is nonpartisan, focusing on the needs and welfare of the city’s residents.

Born into a family with a strong political background, Suarez is the son of former Miami mayor and former Miami-Dade County Commissioner Xavier Suarez. His father’s experience and influence in local politics undoubtedly played a role in shaping Suarez’s own aspirations and commitment to public service.

Aside from his mayoral responsibilities, Francis Suarez has taken on various leadership roles within Miami’s governance and transportation planning. He serves as the vice-chair of the Miami-Dade Transportation Planning Organization (TPO) and has been the president of the US Conference of Mayors from June 2022 to June 2023, following his previous roles as Second Vice President and First Vice President of the organization.

Throughout his tenure as mayor, Francis Suarez has been a champion for numerous initiatives aimed at enhancing the quality of life in Miami. This initiative focuses on combating rising sea levels and supporting the development of affordable housing, addressing critical issues facing the city.

Under his leadership, Miami has witnessed the introduction of innovative solutions such as an online permitting system and a historically low homicide rate, contributing to the overall progress and well-being of the city’s residents.
