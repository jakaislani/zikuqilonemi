Guy Madison was an American actor born on January 19, 1922 in Pumpkin Center, CA. He was best known for his role as Wild Bill Hickok in the 1950s TV series, which made him a household name among baby boomers. Throughout his four-decade career, Madison was recognized for his work with the 1986 Golden Boot Award and a Special Golden Globe Award for Best Western Star.
