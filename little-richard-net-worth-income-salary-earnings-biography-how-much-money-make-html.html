Richard Wayne Penniman was born on the 5th of December, 1932, in Macon, Georgia, United States of America. Since 1947, he has been well known by his stage name Little Richard, and he has been a prominent figure in the music business in the United States for more than six decades. He is best known as a musician, composer, and recording artist whose music has had an impact on a large number of artists and vocalists who have performed a wide range of music from soul to rap. 

Richard’s accomplishments have been recognised numerous times by various organisations, with the most significant honours being his induction into the Songwriters Hall of Fame and the Rock and Roll Hall of Fame, as well as Lifetime Achievement Awards from the Rhythm and Blues Foundation and The Recording Academy.
