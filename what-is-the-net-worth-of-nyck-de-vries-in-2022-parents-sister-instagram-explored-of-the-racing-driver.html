What Is The Net Worth Of Nyck de Vries? Parents &amp; Sister Instagram Explored Of The Racing Driver

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Tuesday, May 14, 2024

Nyck de Vries is a well-known Dutch racing driver whose net worth has recently sparked debate among netizens. Here is what we know about professional racing drivers.

Nyck de Vries, born in Uitwellingerga, Netherlands, is a professional Dutch racing driver currently appearing in the 2021-22 Formula E season with Mercedes-EQ Formula E Team.

De Vries is also a test and reserves driver for the Mercedes-AMG Petronas F1 Team. The racing driver is the winner of the 2020-21 Formula E World Championship and the 2019 FIA Formula 2 Championship.

In addition to his victory, he is also the winner of the 2010 and 2011 Karting World Championships.

Net Worth Of Nyck de Vries In 2022

The professional Dutch racing driver might also get a decent amount from being a reserve driver for the Mercedes F1 team. He was also signed to the McLaren young driver program from January 2010 until May 2019.

Moreover, he was signed to the Audi Sport racing academy from October 2016 until August 2019. All the endeavors that Nyck has been a part of would surely make up a large amount of his net worth.

Nyck de Vries Parents Details: Meet His Father Hendrik Jan de Vries And Mother Naomi Hesseling de Vries

Nyck de Vries was born on February 6, 1995, to his loving parents Hendrik Jan de Vries and Naomi Hessling de Vries. His father is best known for being a car dealership owner.

He won the WSK World Series for the KF3 category and the German Junior Championship in 2008. The following year in 2008, the racing driver retained both his German Junior and WSK World Series titles.

In 2012, he transferred to single-seaters, being provided with a cockpit in the Eurocup Formula 2.0 at R-ace GP. He grabbed his first podium finish at his debit race in Alcaniz.

The following year in 2013, he switched to Koiranen GP for his second season and one race each at the Hungaroring and the Circuit de Catalunya and finished the season in fifth place in the championship.

Does Nyck de Vries Has Sister – Who Is Seychelle de Vries?

Nyck de Vries is the elder child of his parents Hendrik Jan de Vries and Naomi Hessling de Vries. Hendrick and Naomi had two children together. Nyck was raised alongside his younger sister Seychelle de Vries.

The racing drover finished his third season of the Eurocup with Koiranen. He got victory over six games and further finished on the podium in 11 of 14 races.

De Vries later switched to the Formula Renault 3.5 Series, racing for DAMS in 2015. He won the podium five times, eventually winning the final race of the season at the Circuito de Jerez, putting him in third place in the final standings.
