Kristen Miranda Biography

Kristen Miranda is an American journalist working at WBTV. As of now, Kristen is up for her job as the co-host of the show, “QC @ 3” on WBTV. The show premiered on 9 September 2019. She is best known for co-hosting with Coach LaMonte Odums at WBTV’s lifestyle talk show, Morning Break, which airs every weekday at 9 am. As part of this morning’s Emmy-nominated WBTV News, Kristen also brings WBTV viewers breaking news from the Alert Center every morning.

Kristen was recognized for her consumer reporting by the North Carolina Associated Press and the Carolinas Radio Television Digital News Association. Prior to coming to WBTV, she was also honored by the New York State Associated Press and the Syracuse Press Club for her coverage of breaking news.

Kristen Miranda Career

Miranda started her career in journalism as a newsroom assistant for WKBW TV in 1996. With enough experience of nearly two years in the WKBW TV, Kristen got the offer to work as an anchor and reporter in WIVT-TV/WBGH-TV where she worked for three full years.

Before her job in the WBTV, Kristen worked in many other networks like, Element K, 13WHAM-TV and Raycom Media. In her initial days in the WBTV, Kristen worked as a consumer reporter, weekend mornings and weekdays noon anchor. Later, she shifted into Alert Center Anchor and Consumer Investigator, and then finally became the co-host Morning Break.
