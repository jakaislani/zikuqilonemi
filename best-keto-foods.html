9 Best Keto Foods That Actually Taste Good

Posted by Martina Birk on Tuesday, July 16, 2024

When we talk about the best keto foods over here at Sporked, we’re talking about the best tasting keto foods. We might not be nutritionists, but we know what foods taste good—keto or no. So, when we’re tasting keto groceries like keto bread, keto chips, keto bars, etc., we’re evaluating them based on how enjoyable they are to eat. 

The keto food industry is seemingly booming. You can get the keto version of pretty much any food now—ice cream, bagels, salad dressing. There are even chicken skin chips now sold under the keto label. What we’re saying is, it’s a great time to be keto. You have options. But, unfortunately, a lot of them are just plain bad. We’ve tasted so many keto foods. Way more than we ever thought even existed. So let us advise you on the best keto food to buy. Here’s all the best keto snacks and more that we’ve tried throughout our taste tests. 

Best Keto Bars

Elevation Salted Caramel Keto BarInstacart

This keto bar is the closest thing you can get to a candy bar while still keeping within keto-friendly constraints. “It smells like a caramel macchiato from Starbucks,” senior staff writer Jordan Myrick wrote in their ranking of the best keto bars. “It’s sweet, caramelly, and chocolatey.” And the texture is soft and pleasant unlike many of the other keto bars we tried, which were oddly sandy.

Best Keto Bread

Sola Sweet OatWalmart

This keto bread actually tastes like bread. Amazing. That’s because it’s made with wheat gluten, wheat starch, and wheat protein. So, no, it’s not gluten-free. But it still qualifies as keto with just 1g net carbs. Perfect for keto toast, keto sandwiches, keto bread crumbs—all your keto bread needs.

Best Keto Ice Cream

For whatever reason, keto ice cream is better when it’s on a stick. It seems to be creamier. “Halo Top’s Sea Salt Caramel ice cream pop has a lovely, smooth-ish (for keto ice cream) texture, and a delightful salted caramel taste,” Danny wrote in his ranking of the best keto ice cream. Best Keto Cereal

Catalina Crunch Cinnamon Toast Keto Friendly CerealAmazon

This extremely crunchy keto cereal will stand up to milk. You don’t have to worry about it getting soggy. It’s slightly sweeter than classic Cinnamon Toast Crunch, but it’s just as toasty and cinnamon-y. It’s an excellent cereal to keep on hand, whether you’re keto or not.
