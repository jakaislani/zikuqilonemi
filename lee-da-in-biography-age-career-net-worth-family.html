Lee Da-in Biography, Age, Career, Net Worth, Family

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Friday, May 17, 2024

Lee Da-in biography-South Korean actress, Lee Da-in, born Im Joo-hee was born on November 5th, 1992 in Seoul, South Korea.

She was born to Kyeon Mi-ri and Lee Hong-heon. She was adopted by her parents alongside her sister, Lee Yu-bi.

Before getting married, Lee changed her last name from Lee Joo-hee to Lee Ra-yoon. She did not, however, alter her stage name.

Lee-Da-in nationality

Lee-Da-in net worth

Lee-Da-in education

Lee-Da-in career

Lee made her acting debut in 2014 with the tvN drama ‘Twenty Years Old’. 

She continued to perform in numerous dramas, including Make A Woman Cry, Entourage, Hwarang: The Poet Youth Warrior, My Golden Life, Come and Hug Me, Doctor Prison, and Alice. Currently, she is a part of the cast of the upcoming drama Lovers, which also stars Namgoong Min, Ahn Eun Jin, and Lee Hak Joo.

The narrative of a lover who was abandoned in the Byeongja Horan’s squalor and the days they so dearly missed can be found in the film “Lovers.”

Lee takes on the role of Kyung Eun Ae, the fiancee of Nam Yeon Jun (Lee Hak Joo) and the closest friend of Yoo Gil Chae (Ahn Eun Jin).

Kyung Eun Ae is a wise and benevolent woman who knows how to see the bright side of her world first.

Via her mother’s side, Lee Da In is descended from the Hwanggan Kyeon clan, making her a descendant of Gyeon Hwon, the first ruler of the Hubaekje kingdom during the Later Three Kingdoms of Korea.

ESTJ is her MBTI type. According to her and everyone around her, she is the ideal planner, and when she travels, she frequently makes detailed plans.
