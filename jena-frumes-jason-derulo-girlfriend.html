Posted by Aldo Pusey on Monday, May 27, 2024

JENA Frumes shot to fame when she dated England ace Jesse Lingard.

She was then in a relationship with superstar singer Jason Derulo and they welcomed their first child together in May 2021, but the duo has now broken up.

Who is Jena Frumes?

The 27-year-old is originally from New Jersey, but moved to Los Angeles to pursue her dreams of becoming an actress.

Yet, she also has a top education behind her, having graduated in theatre and dance from North Carolina Central University.

Jena got her big break after appearing on American comedy show Wild N' Out, hosted by Mariah Carey's ex-husband Nick Cannon.

Since then, she has racked up over four million followers on Instagram.
